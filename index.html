Joe Academic


Hi, I'm Joe Academic, a post doctoral fellow at University College London, where I'm part of the Big Numbers group led by Needa Prime.

I'm interested in the arithmetic and algorithmic aspects of highly massive numerics. My PhD focused on the problem of naming big numbers, and my more recent work centers on the Adding Big Numbers Conjecture.
